About

Ciat Conlin is a freelancer editor, DP, and director based in Chicago, Illinois. With a passion for telling impactful stories, Ciat has worked across genres including documentaries, narrative films, animation, music videos, and corporate projects.

With experience filming in Poland, Ukraine, France, and the U.S., Ciat's work spans international borders, capturing powerful narratives from diverse perspectives.

Ciat’s approach to shooting and editing focuses on capturing the unique essence of each project, while seamlessly integrating and exploring new technologies and innovative approaches to storytelling.”
